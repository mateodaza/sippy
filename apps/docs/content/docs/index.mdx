**Send and hold digital dollars from WhatsApp.**
No seed phrases. No new app to learn. Start in minutes.

## How it works

1. **Verify your phone** -- get a wallet tied to your number
2. **Add funds** -- deposit USDC to your wallet address
3. **Send money** -- message Sippy on WhatsApp or use the web wallet

## Why people trust Sippy

- **You own your money** -- Sippy never holds your funds. Your wallet is yours.
- **It just works** -- send money like you send a message. No crypto knowledge needed.
- **Transparent** -- every transaction is verifiable on-chain. No hidden fees.
