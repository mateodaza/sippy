title: Recovery email

## Why add an email?

Your email does two things:

1. **Raises your daily limit from $50 to $500.** Without a verified email, your
   daily spending cap stays at $50. Verifying unlocks up to $500.
2. **Protects your account.** If you lose access to your phone number or your
   WhatsApp, a verified email gives you a way back in.

## How to add your email

1. Go to [sippy.lat/settings](https://www.sippy.lat/settings)
2. Find the **Email** section
3. Enter your email address and tap **Save**
4. Check your inbox for a verification link
5. Click the link -- done

The whole process takes about 30 seconds.

## What happens after verification

- Your daily limit cap increases to **$500** (you still choose the exact amount
  in Settings)
- Your email is linked to your account for recovery purposes
- Nothing else changes -- your wallet address stays the same, your balance stays
  the same

## What if I skip it?

Everything still works. You can send and receive money, check your balance, and
use all of Sippy's features. The only differences:

- Your daily limit stays capped at **$50**
- You have no backup way to recover your account

We recommend adding one. It takes 30 seconds and gives you both peace of mind and
a higher limit.

## Can I change my email later?

Yes. Go to [Settings](https://www.sippy.lat/settings) and update it anytime.
You'll need to verify the new address before it takes effect.
