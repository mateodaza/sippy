## What you get

A digital dollar wallet tied to your phone number. You can send and receive money
through WhatsApp or the web -- no app to download, no passwords to remember.

## Step by step

### 1. Open sippy.lat

Go to [sippy.lat](https://www.sippy.lat) on your phone or computer. Tap **Get started**.

### 2. Enter your phone number

Type your full phone number with country code (like +57 for Colombia or +52 for Mexico).
You'll receive a verification code via WhatsApp. If WhatsApp delivery fails, you'll get
an SMS instead.

Enter the code to confirm it's really you.

### 3. Your wallet is ready

That's it. Sippy creates a wallet tied to your phone number. You'll see your **wallet
address** -- a long string starting with `0x`. This is where you'll send funds later.

You don't need to memorize or write down the address. It's always visible on your
wallet page at [sippy.lat/wallet](https://www.sippy.lat/wallet).

### 4. Pick a daily limit

Sippy asks you to set a daily spending limit. This cap applies to sends you make
through WhatsApp and "Free gas" mode on the web. It protects you in case someone
else gets access to your WhatsApp.

Start with whatever feels comfortable. You can change it anytime in
[Settings](https://www.sippy.lat/settings).

## How long does it take?

Under 2 minutes from start to finish. Most people finish in about 60 seconds.
