## Two ways to send

You can send money through **WhatsApp** (just message Sippy) or the **web wallet**
at [sippy.lat/wallet](https://www.sippy.lat/wallet). Both move real USDC -- same
money, same speed, same recipient.

---

## Sending via WhatsApp

Open your chat with Sippy and type what you want to send. Sippy understands
natural language in English, Spanish, and Portuguese.

### Examples

**English:**
> send 10 to +573001234567

**Spanish:**
> enviar 10 a +573001234567

**Portuguese:**
> enviar 10 para +573001234567

You can also send just part of the info and Sippy will ask for the rest:

> send 25

Sippy will reply: "Who do you want to send it to?"

> +573001234567

And it continues from there.

### Confirmation

For any send above $5, Sippy asks you to confirm before executing. You'll see
the amount, the recipient's phone number, and a "yes or no?" prompt. Nothing
moves until you confirm.

Sends of $5 or less execute right away to keep small transfers fast.

### What the recipient sees

The recipient gets a WhatsApp message from Sippy telling them they received money.
The message includes the amount and a link to their wallet. If the recipient isn't
on Sippy yet, see [Invite someone](/docs/using-sippy/invite-someone) for what happens.

### Gas fees

Sippy covers the network fee (gas) for all WhatsApp sends. You send $10, the
recipient gets $10. The gas cost comes from Sippy, not your balance.

## Sending via web wallet

Go to [sippy.lat/wallet](https://www.sippy.lat/wallet) and tap **Send**. Enter the
recipient's phone number and the amount.

You'll see two modes:

### Free gas mode

Sippy submits the transaction and covers the gas fee -- same as WhatsApp. This
counts toward your [daily limit](/docs/using-sippy/limits).

### Direct mode

You submit the transaction yourself using your connected wallet. You pay a small
gas fee (typically less than $0.01 on Arbitrum). This does **not** count toward
your daily limit, so there's no cap.

Direct mode is useful when you've hit your daily limit or want to send larger
amounts without restrictions.

---

## Quick reference

| Method | Gas fee | Daily limit applies? |
|---|---|---|
| WhatsApp | Sippy pays | Yes |
| Web -- Free gas | Sippy pays | Yes |
| Web -- Direct | You pay (< $0.01) | No |
