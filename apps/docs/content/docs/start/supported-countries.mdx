## Where Sippy works

Sippy works with phone numbers from most countries. You can send USDC to anyone who
has a phone number and sets up a wallet.

## Local currency display

When checking your balance, Sippy shows the equivalent in your local currency based
on your phone prefix:

| Country | Prefix | Currency | Example |
|---|---|---|---|
| Colombia | +57 | COP | $42,500.00 COP |
| Mexico | +52 | MXN | $172.50 MXN |
| Brazil | +55 | BRL | R$ 50.00 |
| Argentina | +54 | ARS | $8,500.00 ARS |
| Chile | +56 | CLP | $8,200 CLP |
| Peru | +51 | PEN | S/ 37.50 |
| Venezuela | +58 | VES | Bs. 365.00 |
| Ecuador | +593 | USD | $10.00 |
| United States | +1 | USD | $10.00 |

Exchange rates update every 24 hours.

## Languages

| Phone prefix | Default language |
|---|---|
| +57, +52, +54, +56, +51, +58, +593 | Spanish |
| +55 | Portuguese |
| +1 and others | English |

You can switch your language anytime by messaging Sippy in a different language or
changing it in Settings.

## Restricted regions

Sippy is not available in countries under international sanctions. Phone numbers
from restricted regions may be unable to complete setup. We are working on
formalizing these controls -- if you encounter issues signing up from your region,
please contact support.
