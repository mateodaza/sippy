---
title: What is Sippy?
description: A WhatsApp money agent for Latin America.
---

Sippy lets you send and hold digital dollars using WhatsApp. No bank account needed,
no crypto knowledge required, no new app to install.

You message Sippy like you'd message a friend: "enviar 20 a +573001234567". The money
arrives in seconds. The recipient gets a WhatsApp notification.

## Who is Sippy for?

- People who send money to family across borders
- Freelancers who get paid in dollars and want to hold value
- Anyone in Latin America who wants a dollar-denominated account without a bank

## What Sippy is not

Sippy is not a bank, not an exchange, and not a custodian. It's a tool that gives
you a wallet you own and a simple way to use it. Your money sits in your wallet on
Arbitrum -- Sippy helps you interact with it, but can't touch it without your permission.

## The basics

- **Currency:** USDC -- a digital dollar backed 1:1 by US dollars
- **Network:** Arbitrum -- a fast, low-cost Ethereum network
- **Wallet:** A smart account you control, created when you sign up
- **Interface:** WhatsApp for everyday use, web wallet for full control
