title: Get started
description: Set up your wallet in under 2 minutes.
---

Setting up Sippy takes about two minutes. All you need is your phone — the one with
WhatsApp. No app to install, no documents, no crypto knowledge.

## 1. Open sippy.lat

Go to [sippy.lat](https://www.sippy.lat) on your phone or computer. You can also start
by messaging Sippy on WhatsApp — it will send you the same setup link.

## 2. Verify your phone number

Enter your phone number. You'll receive a 6-digit code on WhatsApp (or by SMS as a
fallback). Enter it to confirm it's you. Your phone number *is* your account — there's
no username or password to remember.

## 3. Your wallet is created

Sippy instantly creates a wallet tied to your phone number. It's self-custodial —
yours, not ours — so only you can move the money in it. You'll see your wallet
address; that's where digital dollars (USDC) land when you add funds.

## 4. Set your daily limit

Choose a daily limit for WhatsApp sends. Think of it as a safety cap: if someone ever
got into your WhatsApp, this is the most they could move in a day. Start small — you
can change it anytime, and adding a [recovery email](/docs/using-sippy/recovery-email)
unlocks higher limits.

## 5. Add funds

There are a few ways to put dollars in your wallet:

- **From the [Fund page](https://fund.sippy.lat)** — the simplest way to top up your own number.
- **Ask a friend on Sippy** — have them send to your phone number; it arrives in seconds.
- **Already hold crypto?** — send USDC on the Arbitrum network to your wallet address (shown on your wallet page).

Once the dollars land, you're ready to send.

## 6. Send your first transfer

Open WhatsApp and message Sippy:

> send 10 to +573001234567

Or in Spanish:

> enviar 10 a +573001234567

Sippy shows you the details and asks you to confirm. You say yes, and the money arrives
in seconds — Sippy covers the network fee, so there's nothing extra to pay.
