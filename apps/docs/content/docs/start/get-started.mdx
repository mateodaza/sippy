---
title: Get started
description: Set up your wallet in under 2 minutes.
---

## 1. Go to sippy.lat

Open [sippy.lat](https://www.sippy.lat) on your phone or computer.

## 2. Verify your phone number

Enter your phone number. You'll receive a verification code via WhatsApp (or SMS
as a fallback). Enter the code to prove it's you.

## 3. Your wallet is created

Sippy creates a wallet tied to your phone number. You'll see your wallet address --
this is where you'll send USDC to fund your account.

## 4. Set your daily limit

Choose a daily spending limit for WhatsApp sends. This protects you in case someone
else gets access to your WhatsApp. You can change it anytime.

## 5. Add funds

Send USDC on Arbitrum to your wallet address. You can find it on the wallet page
or the fund page. Once it arrives, you're ready to send.

## 6. Send your first transfer

Open WhatsApp and message Sippy:

> send 10 to +573001234567

Or in Spanish:

> enviar 10 a +573001234567

Sippy confirms, you say yes, and the money arrives in seconds.
