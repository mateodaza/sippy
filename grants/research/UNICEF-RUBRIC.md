- Not about "Arbitrum Everywhere" — about **financial inclusion for families**
- WhatsApp = reaches populations without bank accounts or app stores
- Non-custodial = users own their funds, no intermediary diversion
- On-chain = full audit trail, transparent, verifiable
- Aid disbursement potential: UNICEF sends USDC → arrives in recipient's WhatsApp wallet → verifiable on Arbiscan
- Open source = aligns with digital public goods mandate
