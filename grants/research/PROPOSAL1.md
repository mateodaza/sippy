Sippy is the first Web3 payment infrastructure that enables users to send, receive, and spend stablecoins directly through WhatsApp-no app downloads, no seed phrases, no crypto knowledge required.

<br/>OUR SOLUTION:  
• Users send WhatsApp messages like "Send \$50 to mom"  
• LLM engine processes natural language commands in Spanish/Portuguese/English  
• Account Abstraction (ERC-4337) creates wallets automatically on first transfer
