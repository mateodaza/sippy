## 🎯 **Delivery Tips**

### **Energy & Pacing:**

- **Start calm, build energy** - Problem is serious, solution is exciting
- **Slow down for the GIF** - Let each moment land, don't rush the narration
- **Speed up slightly for tech stack** - You're confident, this is proven infrastructure
- **End with conviction** - "This is how we bring the next billion users to crypto"

### **Hand Gestures:**

- **Slide 4** - Point to each feature as you mention it
- **Slide 5** - Keep hands relaxed, gesture to screen as GIF plays
- **Live demo** - Don't talk with your hands while typing/clicking

### **Eye Contact:**

- Look at camera/audience 70% of the time
- Glance at slides 30% to reference specific points
- **During GIF** - Look at audience and narrate, not at screen

### **Vocal Variation:**

- **"Everyone in LatAm uses WhatsApp"** - matter of fact
- **"We make crypto invisible"** - confident, bold
- **During GIF** - conversational, like you're explaining to a friend
- **"This is real. It's on-chain."** - emphatic, proving it works
- **Final line** - slow down, let it resonate
