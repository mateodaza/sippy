## 1. Usage-led beats farm-led

**The positive comps — incentives that amplified a product that already worked:**

- **Hyperliquid — the strongest usage-led comp.** Points amplified real perps trading _after_ the product worked; post-airdrop metrics stayed strong, HLP TVL rose from ~$150M to $400M+ into early 2025 ([OAK Research](https://oakresearch.io/en/reports/protocols/hyperliquid-hype-s1-2025-activity-report)). Points were based on trading/referral activity to growing cohorts of _actual users_, not passive depositors ([Privy case study](https://privy.io/blog/hyperliquid-case-study)).
- **Jupiter — eligibility tied to real swap volume, not idle deposits.** First JUP phase targeted wallets with ≥$1,000 historical swap volume ([The Block](https://www.theblock.co/post/265757/solana-dex-aggregator-jupiter-starts-token-airdrop-with-10-of-supply-set-to-be-allocated)); later shifted toward fee-paying users + active governance ([BlockEden](https://blockeden.xyz/blog/2026/01/26/jupiter-jupuary-final-airdrop-jupusd-stablecoin-solana-defi/)).

**The cautionary comps — passive/deposit farms that collapsed:**

- **Blast:** TVL −~65% in 24h post-mainnet, ~$2.27B → ~$745M ([CryptoSlate](https://cryptoslate.com/blast-network-tvl-plummets-65-losing-2-billion-in-24-hours-post-mainnet-launch/)); ~−60% within two months of the airdrop, BLAST ~−91% from ATH ([The Defiant](https://thedefiant.io/news/blockchains/blast-tvl-plunges-another-30-as-users-abandon-the-network)).
- **friend.tech:** deposits $52M → $4M; daily new users → single digits; daily fees ~$2M → <$100 ([DL News](https://www.dlnews.com/articles/defi/friend-tech-shuts-down-after-revenue-and-users-plummet/)).
- **Berachain:** Boyco >$1.5B pre-launch deposits ([Berachain](https://blog.berachain.com/blog/solving-the-cold-start-problem-exploring-the-synergies-between-boyco-and-pre-launch-vaults-2)) → ~$1.1B+ net outflows, >70% TVL decline ("ghost chain") ([BeInCrypto](https://beincrypto.com/berachain-outflows-turned-ghost-chain/)).
- **EigenLayer:** points created expectation risk — a high-TVL "lockbox" before full product functionality; framed as a warning for points programs ([CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/tech/2024/05/09/eigenlayers-eigen-airdrop-might-signal-demise-of-once-popular-points)).

**Takeaway:** rewards are not the enemy — _passive/deposit_ rewards are. Gate on real usage (Hyperliquid/Jupiter), distribute to growing cohorts of actual users, and the incentive amplifies PMF instead of faking it.

## 2. Reputation / usage-score evidence

- **Gitcoin Passport / Human Passport** — strongest non-token reputation pattern. Passport scores weight/qualify participants; reduced flagged addresses ~60% in GG23 ([Gitcoin](https://gitcoin.co/research/quadratic-funding-sybil-resistance)); now Sybil-resistance/reputation infra with 2M+ users ([Human Passport](https://passport.human.tech/)).
- **Farcaster Power Badge** — useful caution: labels higher-quality active users, but only ~4,360 daily Power Badge users vs ~55,000 DAUs — reputation scores identify quality cohorts, ≠ total adoption ([The Defiant](https://thedefiant.io/news/nfts-and-web3/how-many-users-does-farcaster-actually-have)).
- **Grass** — good usage-score _mechanic_: referral bonuses unlock only after 100 hours of uptime; score rewards network contribution, not signup ([Grass docs](https://grass-foundation.gitbook.io/grass-docs/how-to-guide/grass-points)). _Flag: Grass later tokenized points — use as mechanics evidence, not "non-token" proof._

## 3. LATAM distribution beyond CT

- **WhatsApp is the channel:** 80–90% of Latin Americans use WhatsApp daily — a platform advantage ([QED](https://www.qedinvestors.com/blog/why-we-invested-in-felix-pago)).
- **Félix validates WhatsApp remittances:** WhatsApp + stablecoin rails, ~$3B payment volume, 30% MoM growth, ~90% authorization ([Stripe](https://stripe.com/customers/felix)); grew 175k → 400k users, expanded to Colombia, much growth via referrals ([Refresh Miami](https://refreshmiami.com/news/felix-is-turning-whatsapp-into-a-remittance-engine-for-400000-immigrants/)).
- **Corridors are already stablecoin-relevant:** Bitso processed >$6.5B US–Mexico remittances in 2024, >10% of the corridor ([Bitso](https://business.bitso.com/en/blog/stablecoins-in-action-the-new-financial-edge-for-global-enterprises)).
- **Demand is utility-driven:** LATAM ~$1.5T crypto volume Jul 2022–Jun 2025 (inflation, currency volatility, capital controls, remittances) ([Chainalysis 2025](https://www.chainalysis.com/blog/latin-america-crypto-adoption-2025/)); Argentina stablecoin share 61.8% vs 44.7% global ([Chainalysis 2024](https://www.chainalysis.com/blog/2024-latin-america-crypto-adoption/)).
- **Community/events (thinner, supportive):** active Colombia groups — Ethereum Bogotá/Cali/Caribe, Bitcoin Wednesday Bogotá ([Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/topics/smart-contracts/co/)). Channel evidence, not conversion proof.

## 4. X / paid-posting risk

- **X developer policy:** services should **not compensate people for X actions** — posts, reposts, likes, comments, replies ([X Developer Policy](https://docs.x.com/developer-terms/policy)).
- **Kaito/Yaps cautionary case:** X moved against apps paying users to post; Kaito sunset Yaps, its Yapper community was banned, KAITO −~17% ([CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/business/2026/01/15/kaito-to-sunset-yaps-as-x-cracks-down-on-infofi-apps-token-falls-17)).
- **Allowed/risk split:** paid creator partnerships can exist; pay-to-post farming / programmatic engagement rewards cannot. FTC requires clear disclosure of material connections — money, free products, discounts, perks ([FTC Disclosures 101](https://www.ftc.gov/business-guidance/resources/disclosures-101-social-media-influencers)).

## 5. Stablecoin "WhatsApp moment"

- **Verified, Sippy-exact:** sending $200 US→Colombia costs $12.13 traditionally vs $0.01 with stablecoins (excluding fiat conversion spreads/fees) ([a16z, Apr 9 2025](https://a16zcrypto.com/posts/article/stablecoins-payments-without-intermediaries/)).
- **The "WhatsApp moment" piece:** stablecoins moved **>$12T** after filtering bots/inorganic activity, approaching Visa's ~$17T; users will increasingly just feel like they're "using dollars" ([a16z, Feb 13 2026](https://a16zcrypto.com/posts/article/stablecoins-whatsapp-moment-money/)).
- **Use:** CT credibility with builders/investors/KOLs; actual acquisition centers WhatsApp, diaspora corridors, trusted local networks.

## 6. Referral mechanics that create active users (gate on a real milestone)

- **Grass:** referral bonus unlocks only after **100 hours of referee uptime** ([Grass docs](https://grass-foundation.gitbook.io/grass-docs/how-to-guide/grass-points)).
- **Gemini:** referred user must **trade ≥$100 within 30 days** ([Gemini terms](https://www.gemini.com/legal/gemini-referral-rewards-program-terms)).
- **Crypto.com:** referral rewards credited **after trading milestones** (some require a 30-day condition) ([Crypto.com](https://help.crypto.com/en/articles/3124990-app-referral-programme)).
- **Interactive Brokers (fintech analogue):** referrer paid only if the referred client **deposits $10,000 and maintains it for one year** ([IBKR](https://www.interactivebrokers.com/en/trading/referral-member-to-member.php)).

## Uncertain / flagged

- Direct evidence that _non-token reputation scores alone_ drive retained crypto consumer growth is limited. The stronger, well-supported claim: reputation/personhood scores **reduce Sybil abuse and improve reward quality.**
- KOL pricing and "best time to post" data are agency-reported — directional only.
- LATAM community/event channels are credible but under-quantified; **Félix's WhatsApp/referral data is the strongest concrete non-CT distribution proof.**
