05 // Target Market

We're starting in Latin America because that's where it's needed most.

  • Freelancers and remote workers
    Paid in USD, need to move money fast across borders
  • Families sending remittances
    International transfers without bank fees
  • Small businesses
    Accept digital dollar payments with zero infrastructure
  • Savers in unstable economies
    Protection against devaluation in digital dollars
  • First market: Colombia
    50M people, 95% WhatsApp penetration, high USD demand
  • Expansion: Mexico, Argentina, Brazil
    Same infrastructure, multiple local currencies
