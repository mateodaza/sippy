The Problem

Andrea is a graphic designer in Medellín.

Her brother in Bogotá owes her for splitting rent. A bank transfer takes a day and costs fees on both sides. A remittance app charges 3-7%. Cash requires someone to physically go to a point.

Stablecoins could solve this: a dollar-denominated transfer that settles in seconds for fractions of a cent. But today, using stablecoins means downloading a wallet app, managing a seed phrase, buying ETH for gas, and navigating interfaces built for traders.

Andrea isn't going to do any of that. Neither will her brother. Neither will the millions of people across LATAM in the same situation.

The Solution

Sippy lets users send and hold stablecoins through WhatsApp, without downloading a wallet app or managing gas.

The user texts a message in their own language. The AI agent parses it, creates a non-custodial wallet if needed, sponsors gas, executes the transfer on-chain, and confirms back in the same chat. All in seconds.

Supports Spanish, Portuguese, and English. Understands slang and casual input across Colombian, Mexican, Argentine, and Venezuelan dialects.

Non-custodial. Users control their own wallet and keys.

Trust & Security

Security & Trust

Non-Custodial

Users own their wallets and keys. Sippy never holds funds. Wallets are powered by Coinbase CDP with optional private key export.

Transaction Controls

Confirmation prompts above $5. Velocity limits: 5 sends per 10 min, $500/hr cap, 3 new recipients/hr. Tiered daily limits ($50 unverified, $500 with email).

Fraud Prevention

Spam detection, rate limiting per user and IP, self-send blocking, anti-injection validation on all AI inputs, full audit logging.

Fast Settlement

Transfers settle on Arbitrum in seconds. Gas is sponsored through a custom smart contract so users never need to hold ETH.

Messaging Infrastructure

WhatsApp Business API, production-approved by Meta. Reliable delivery with template notifications for payments and receipts.

Monitoring

PostHog analytics, on-chain indexer for real-time transaction monitoring, admin dashboard for user management.

Compliance & Risk

Compliance & Risk Controls

Custody

Non-custodial. Wallets are created via Coinbase CDP. Sippy never holds user funds or private keys. Users can export keys and sweep funds at any time.

Jurisdictions

Whitelist model: only allowed jurisdictions can onboard. US, EU, FATF high-risk, and sanctioned countries are blocked at onboarding (IP + phone prefix). Currently scoped to Colombia.

Identity & Limits

Phone verification at onboarding (Twilio OTP). Tiered daily limits: $50 unverified, $500 with email. Per-transaction cap of $100. Velocity limits on send frequency and new recipients.

Fraud & AML

Rate limiting per user and IP. Spam detection via LLM validator. Self-send blocking. Full audit trail on all transfers and key exports. Admin can block users and pause the system.

Legal Entity

Established in El Salvador. Co-founder Lina Sandoval (Fintech & Regulatory Counsel, former Bancolombia) leads compliance strategy across LATAM jurisdictions.

Positioning

Sippy is a technology interface for interacting with wallets on blockchain. It does not custody funds or offer regulated financial services. Landing page and all user-facing copy reviewed for regulatory alignment.
